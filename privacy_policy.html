Rep AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 March 2026

Rep AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

No Personal Data Collection

Rep AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not require an account, login, or any personal information to use the app.

On-Device AI Processing

All pose detection and form analysis is performed entirely on your device using on-device machine learning. No video, images, or camera data is ever recorded, stored, or sent to any server. Your camera feed is processed in real time and immediately discarded.

Local Data Storage

Your workout history, streak data, and app preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your phone and is not accessible to us or any third party.

No Analytics or Tracking

Rep AI does not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or tracking technologies. We do not track your usage, behaviour, or location.

No Advertising

Rep AI does not display any advertisements and does not share data with any advertising networks.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services. It functions entirely offline.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info.ohmslabs@gmail.com
