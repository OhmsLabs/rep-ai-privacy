Rep AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 April 2026

Rep AI is built by Ohms Labs. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data We Collect

Rep AI offers an optional account feature that allows you to back up your workout history to the cloud. If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address solely to authenticate you and enable this feature. This is the only personal data we collect.

How We Use Your Email Address

Your email address is used only to create and manage your Rep AI account. It is not used for marketing, advertising, or shared with any third party.

Firebase

Account authentication and cloud backup are powered by Google Firebase, a third-party service provided by Google LLC. Firebase may process your email address as part of authentication. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

On-Device AI Processing

All pose detection and form analysis is performed entirely on your device using on-device machine learning. No video, images, or camera data is ever recorded, stored, or sent to any server. Your camera feed is processed in real time and immediately discarded.

Local Data Storage

If you do not create an account, your workout history, streak data, and app preferences are stored locally on your device only and never leave your phone.

No Analytics or Tracking

Rep AI does not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or tracking technologies. We do not track your usage, behaviour, or location.

No Advertising

Rep AI does not display any advertisements and does not share data with any advertising networks.

Camera

Rep AI uses your device camera solely to detect body pose in real time during workouts. Camera access is required for the app to function. No video or images are recorded, saved, or transmitted at any point.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app. Locally stored workout data can be deleted by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Your Rights

If you are located in the United Kingdom or European Union, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

Contact
